## Risk Management

### Mitigation Strategies
1. **Spam Prevention**: 
   - Unique, valuable content for each submission
   - Professional tone always
   - Follow contribution guidelines exactly

2. **Time Investment**:
   - Pre-qualify opportunities (minimum $500)
   - Estimate effort vs. reward ratio
   - Set maximum time per bounty

3. **Payment Risk**:
   - Verify organization legitimacy
   - Check payment history if available
   - Diversify across multiple opportunities
